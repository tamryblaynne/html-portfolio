Tamry Blaynne's Movie Mania

5 Greatest Movies of ALL Time!

Hundred Foot Journey

This is a beautiful story of how two cultures overcame their differences and began to appreciate the gifts of the other through the avenue of their food.

The Last Samauri

From a broken haunted soldier to a man at peace with himself, this story follows the life of a US Army Major who overcame the guilt of the slaughter of the Native Americans, by standing up for the rights of the people of Japan. Hired to train field laborers into soldiers, he was wounded and captured in the first skirmish. High in the mountain village of the Samauri, he was brought back to health and then healed emotionally when he chose to fight for something beyond the guilt of his past. He laid down his life to preserve the beauty and respect of the Samauri life.

The Sound of Music

A timeless classic filled with incredible music and the true account of a nun who became the wife of an Austrian Captain and the mother of his seven children. This recounts their love story and more, their willingness to fight for their freedom and not live their lives under Hilter's oppression.

Howl's Moving Castle

One of the best anime of all time. Beautiful music, enchanting story line.

Ever After

The most beautiful rendition of Cinderella ever created. Danielle never loses her innocence or kindness despite the wickedness of her stepmother/sister. A beautiful love story, filled with humor, adventure, wit, and selfless love.
